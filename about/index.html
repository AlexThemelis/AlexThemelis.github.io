About

I am a MSc student in Pure Mathematics at the University of Athens.

In 2024, I graduated from the Parisian Master of Research in Computer Science (MPRI) at Université Paris Cité.

I did my M2 internship at the Institut de Recherche en Informatique Fondamentale (IRIF). I was lucky to be under the supervision of Geoffroy Couteau and Sihang Pu. My internship was on the development of Threshold signature schemes based on lattice assumptions.

In 2023, I graduated from the Department of Informatics & Telecommunications at the University of Athens.

Aside from mathematics, I’m widely interested in number-theoretic cryptography, as well as various aspects of theoretical computer science.
